<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kya poochtay ho mujh se ke kya kya Hasan ka hai (x2).<br/>Jo kuch bhi hai Khuda ka wo… saara Hasan ka hai.<br/>Kya poochtay ho mujh se…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">1. Aur hum kya hain keh raha hai ye khud deen-e-Mustafa (x2).<br/>Aray mujh ko Ali ke baad… sahara Hasan ka hai.<br/>Kya poochtay ho mujh se…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">2. Tabdeel kar ke jins Hasan ne bata diya (x2).<br/>Ke kehtay hain jis ko kun wo… irada Hasan ka hai.<br/>Kya poochtay ho mujh se…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">3. Wajib samajh ke karta hoon… main tab se laanatain (x2).<br/>Aray jab se meri nazar main… janaaza Hasan ka hai.<br/>Kya poochtay ho mujh se…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">4. Is khush khati se chalti hai… maidaan-e-jang main (x2).<br/>Ke lagta hai Zulfiqar pe… saaya Hasan ka hai.<br/>Kya poochtay ho mujh se…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size"><span style="font-weight: 400;">5. Arzak ki laash dekh kar… boli ye fauj-e-shaam (x2).<br/>Allah ka ghazab hai ke… beta Hasan ka hai.<br/>Main kya bataaon aap ko… kya kya Hasan ka hai.</span></p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">6. Kehnay ko dha diya usay… Aal-e-Saoud ne (x2).<br/>Har dil mein lekin aaj bhi… Roza Hasan ka hai.<br/>Main kya bataaon aap ko… kya kya Hasan ka hai.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">7. Allah se Ali ko… mila ab wirasatan (x2).<br/>Sochain agar to khana-e-kaba Hasan ka hai.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">8. Kyun kar na do jahan mai… tu sarfaraz ho (x2).<br/>Aey Meer tere saath… hawala Hasan ka hai.</p>
