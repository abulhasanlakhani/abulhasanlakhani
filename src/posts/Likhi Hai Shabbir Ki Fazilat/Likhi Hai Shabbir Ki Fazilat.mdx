<p>Likhi Hai Shabbir Ki Fazilat, Kalam Ne Sir Ko Jhuka Jhuka Ke.<br />
Main Is Qaseeday Ki Daad Paaunga, Sayyeda Ko Suna Suna Ke.</p>
<p>Sanaa-e-Shabbir Jab Hun Likhta, Main Pehlay Naad-e-Ali Hoon Padhta.<br />
Zakhira Alfaaz Ka Na Jaane, Kahan Se Na Cheez Ko Hai Milta.<br />
Ye Lafz Jibreel Meri Jaanib, Hai Bhej Deta Saja Saja Ke.</p>
<p><!--more--></p>
<p>Ajeeb Aaamad Ka Silsila Hai, Ke Yun Shah-e-Mashriqain Aaaya.<br />
Khuda Ke Ghar Main Ali Hain Aaye, Ali Ke Ghar Main Hussain Aaya.<br />
Maafiyaan Paa Raha Hai Pitrus, Ab Is Ka Jhula Jhula Jhula Ke.</p>
<p>Haseen Aisa Hua Na hoga, Yehi Hai Kamsin Ali Ke Ghar Main.<br />
Hussain Ik Hai Aur Ik Rahay Ga, Nahi Hai Koi Bhi Behr-o-Bar Main.<br />
Nazar Lagay Na Jabhi To Zehra, Isay Hain Rakhti Chupa Chupa Ke.</p>
<p>Kabhi To Shabbir Ko Nabi Ne, Sawaar Kaandhoan Pe Khud Kiya Hai.<br />
Kabhi Ye Sajday Main Pusht-e-Sarwar, Pe Khud Sawaar Aa Ke Ho Gaya Hai.<br />
Rasool Ne Zindagi Guzari Hai, Naaz Is Ke Utha Utha Ke.</p>
<p>Ali Jo Mushkil Kusha Hai Sab Ka, Hussain Bhi To Hai Sab Ka Mohsin.<br />
Hussainiyat Ke Lagao Naaray, Hussain Ke Sab Jalainge Dushman.<br />
Ali Ke Munkir Ko Bhi Jalao, Ali Ke Naaray Laga Laga Ke.</p>
<p>Hai Dada Imran Ali Padar Hai, Yazeed Se Kyun Hussain Darta.<br />
Hasan Hai Bhai Batool Maa Hai, Zameer Ka Sauda Kaisay Karta.<br />
Nabi Ne Qaisar Isay Hai Paala, Zubaan Apni Chusa Chusa Ke.</p>
