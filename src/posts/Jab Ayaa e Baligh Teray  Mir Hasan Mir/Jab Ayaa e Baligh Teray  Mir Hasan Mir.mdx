<p>Jab ayaa e baligh teray tewar nazar aayay (x2).</p>
<p>Jab ayaa e baligh teray tewar nazar aayay.<br />
Dunya mein Ali sab se kadaawar nazar aayay.<br />
Jab aaya e baligh teray tewar nazar aayay.</p>
<p>1. Suraj ki kahin dhoop kahin ishq ka saaya (x2).<br />
Ik waqt mein do tarhan ke manzar nazar aayay.<br />
Jab aaya e baligh teray tewar nazar aayay.</p>
<p>2. Ik noor ke do tukday thay aaeena e khum mein (x2).<br />
Jis rukh se bhi dekha wo barabar nazar aayay.<br />
Jab aaya e baligh…</p>
<p>3. Aelaan wilaayat ka hazaaron ne suna tha (x2).<br />
Maangi jo shahadat to bahattar nazar aayay.<br />
Jab aaya e baligh…</p>
<p>4. Haathon se mere choot gaya sagar e kausar (x2).<br />
Kausar pe jo khud saaqi e kausar nazar aayay.<br />
Jab aaya e baligh…</p>
<p>5. Allah re ye mojiza e noor e wilaayat (x2).<br />
Andhoan ko bhi maidaan mein Haider nazar aayay.<br />
Jab aaya e baligh…</p>
<p>6. Nabiyon ko jahaan dhoond raha tha main wahan par (x2).<br />
Fizza nazar aayi kabhi kambar nazar aayay.<br />
Jab aaya e baligh…</p>
<p>7. Keh dena ke Behlol hai ye Mir Hasan bhi.<br />
Dunya jo uthaayay huay pathar nazar aayay.<br />
Jab aaya e baligh tere tewar nazar aayay.</p>
