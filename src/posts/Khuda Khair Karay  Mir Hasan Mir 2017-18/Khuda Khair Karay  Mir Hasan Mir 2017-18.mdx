<p class="has-normal-font-size">Khuda khair karay</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka khula sar hai Khuda khair karay.<br/>Bint-e-Zehra ka khula sar hai Khuda khair karay (x2).</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka khula sar hai Khuda khair karey (x2).<br/>Aur har haath mein patthar hai Khuda khair karey.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka… (x2)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Hurr ke betay pe hi Shabbir bohat roye thay (x2).<br/>Ab to laash-e-Ali Akbar hai Khuda khair karey.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka… (x2)  </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Maartay hain wahan naizo’n ko zamee’n par zaalim (x2).<br/>Dafn jis jaa Ali Asghar hai Khuda khair karey.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka… (x2)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kuch kadam duur khadi dekh rahi hai Zainab (x2).<br/>Shimr ke haath mein khanjar hai Khuda khair karey.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka… (x2)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Chonk kar khwaab se kehti thi watan mein Sughra (x2).<br/>Kisi mushkil mein mera ghar hai Khuda khair karey.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka… (x2)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Peechay deewar hai aur jalta hua dar aagay (x2).<br/>Beech mein Bint-e-Payambar hai Khuda khair karey.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka… (x2)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Be rida jaana hai Zainab ko waha’n par ke jahaa’n (x2).<br/>Har koi dushman-e-Haider hai Khuda khair karey.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka… (x2)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Hai chadhi haath mein zaalim ke laboa’n par hai hansi (x2).<br/>Tasht mein sheh ka rakha sar hai Khuda khair karey.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bint-e-Zehra ka… (x2)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Mir Sajjad ye Shiree’n ne kaha rotay huay (x2).<br/>Kis ki nezay pe ye chadar hai Khuda khair karey.</p>
