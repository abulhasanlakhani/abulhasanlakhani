<p class="has-normal-font-size">Faslon ko takalluf hai hum se agar.<br/>Hum bhi be bas nahin be sahara nahi.<br/>Khud unhi ko pukarenge hum duur se.<br/>Raaste mein agar paaon thak jaayenge.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Hum Madinay mein tanha nikal jaayenge.<br/>Aur galiyoan mein kasdan bhatak jaayenge.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Hum wahan ja ke wapas nahin aayenge.<br/>Dhoondte dhoondte log thak jaayenge.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Jaise hi sabz Gumbad nazar aayega.<br/>Bandagi ka kareena badal jaayega.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Sar jhukaane ki fursat milay gi kisey.<br/>Khud hi palkoan se sajday tapak jaayenge.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Naam-e-Aaqa jahan bhi liya jaayega.<br/>Zikr un ka jahan bhi kiya jaayega.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Noor hi noor seenoan mein bhar jaayega.<br/>Saari mehfil mein jalway lapak jaayenge.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Aye Madinay ke zaayir Khuda ke liye.<br/>Dastaan-e-safar mujh ko yoon mat suna.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Baat badh jaayegi dil tadap jaayega.<br/>Meray mohtat aansoo chalak jaayenge.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Un ki chashm-e-karam ko hai is ki khabar.<br/>Kis musafir ko hai kitna shauk-e-safar.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Hum ko Iqbal jab bhi ijaazat mili.<br/>Hum bhi Aaqa ke darbar tak jaayenge.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Faslon ko takalluf hai hum se agar.<br/>Hum bhi be bas nahin be sahara nahi.<br/>Khud unhi ko pukarenge hum duur se.<br/>Raaste mein agar paaon thak jaayenge .</p>
