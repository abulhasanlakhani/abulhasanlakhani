<p dir="ltr">Noor ki anjuman Fatima (x2).<br />
Markaz-e-Panjetan Fatima (x2).</p>
<p dir="ltr">1. Arsh se ban ke aayi hai tu.<br />
Murtaza ki dulhan Fatima.</p>
<p dir="ltr">2. Kehkashan se ziyaada haseen (x2).<br />
Tere ghar ka chaman Fatima.</p>
<p dir="ltr">3. Khuld ka arsh par hai dimaa.<br />
Chhu ke tere charan Fatima.</p>
<p dir="ltr">4. Kab kisi maa ne paida kiye.<br />
Phir Hussain-o-Hasan Fatima.</p>
<p dir="ltr">5. Ban gaya tera baagh-e-fidak.<br />
Ghaasibon ka kafan Fatima.</p>
<p dir="ltr">6. Boli Zainab se rooh-e-Ali.<br />
Sar utha aur ban Fatima.</p>
<p dir="ltr">7. Hai ye Qaul-e-Nabi aye Sarosh.<br />
Mera tan mera man Fatima.</p>
