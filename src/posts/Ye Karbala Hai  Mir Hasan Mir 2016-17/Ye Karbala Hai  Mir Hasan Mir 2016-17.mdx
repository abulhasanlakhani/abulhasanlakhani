<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-cyan-bluish-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">**Ye Karbala Hai Ye Karbala Hai**</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar kadam uthana.<br/>Ye Karbala hai…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Ye baat hargiz na bhool jaana.<br/>Ye Karbala…<br/>Yahan sambhal kar kadam uthana.<br/>Ye Karbala…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Jahaan-e-sabr-o-riza yehi hai.<br/>Khuda-e-sabr-o-riza yehi hai.<br/>Yahan pe aatay hain chal ke Mehdi.<br/>Bahisht-e-Zehra ye sar zamee’n hai.<br/>Yahan hai Zehra ka aana jaana.<br/>Ye Karbala…(x2)<br/></p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar…<br/>Ye Karbala…(x2)</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Duaaein dengay Hussain tum ko.<br/>Yahan pe aansoo agar bahaaye.<br/>Jahan pe beti ne be watan ki.<br/>Sitamgaron se tamaachay khaaye.<br/>Yahan pe khushion ko bhool jaana.<br/>Ye Karbala…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar…<br/>Ye Karbala…(x2)</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan pe guzri hai wo qayaamat.<br/>Mila na is ko sukoon ab tak.<br/>Ridaayain chinnay ke gham mein Abid.<br/>Lehad mein rota hai khoon ab tak.<br/>Yahan pe hargiz na muskurana.<br/>Ye Karbala…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar…<br/>Ye Karbala…(x2) </p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan pe Hurr bhi tha hurmala bhi.<br/>Kitaab-e-hasti ko padh ke dekho.<br/>Sadaa-e-hal min to aa rahi hai.<br/>Tum is taraf ho ya us taraf ho.<br/>Tumhain amal se hai ab batana.<br/>Ye Karbala…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar…<br/>Ye Karbala…(x2)</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Kaheen pe Qasim huay hain tukday.<br/>Kaheen pe zee’n se giray thay Mola.<br/>Kaheen Sakeena sumo’n se liptee.<br/>Kaheen pe Asghar ne teer khaya.<br/>Yaheen gareebi ka hai thikaana.<br/>Ye Karbala…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar…<br/>Ye Karbala…(x2) </p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Sawaari aayegi ik wahaan se.<br/>Najaf ki jaanib nigaah rakhna.<br/>Jab aaye shaam-e-ghareeba’n us dam.<br/>Ali ko Zainab ka pursa dena.<br/>Tum aansuoan ke diye jalaana.<br/>Ye Karbala…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar…<br/>Ye Karbala…(x2)</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Kisi ke dil mein lagi hai barchi.<br/>Kisi ke baazu katay yahee’n par.<br/>Ali ki beti ke kitne Yusuf.<br/>Lahu mein doobay hain is zamee’n par.<br/>Luta hai saadaat ka gharaana.<br/>Ye Karbala…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar…<br/>Ye Karbala…(x2)</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Rahay kahee’n bhi jahaa’n mein jaa kar.<br/>Hussain waalay ye jaantay hain.<br/>Hum ahl-e-maatam ke dil Takallum.<br/>Yahaa’n ki matti se hi banay hain.<br/>Yahaa’n hai waapas palat ke aana.<br/>Ye Karbala…</p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">Yahan sambhal kar kadam uthaana.<br/>Ye Karbala hai (x2).</p>
