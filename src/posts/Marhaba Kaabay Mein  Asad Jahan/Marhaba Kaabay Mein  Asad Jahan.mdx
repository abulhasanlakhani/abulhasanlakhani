<p>Marhaba kaabay mein chamka noor-e-imaan baar chaand.<br />
Dekhiye Allah ke ghar ko lagay ab chaar chaand.<br />
Marhaba kaabay mein chamka.</p>
<p>1. Dekhnay walay mah-o-khursheed yakjaan dekh lain (x2).<br />
Ahmed-e-Mukhtar suraj. Haider-e-Karrar chaand (x2).<br />
Marhaba kaabay mein…</p>
<p>2. Kya samaayega nazar mein jalwa-e-badr-o-hilaal (x2).<br />
Aap ke abroo ma-e-nau. Aap ke rukhsaar chaand (x2).<br />
Marhaba kaabay mein…</p>
<p>3. Murtaza-o-Fatima Hasnain noor-e-do jahan (x2).<br />
Ik rida-e-Mustafa mein jalwagar hain chaar chaand (x2).</p>
<p>Marhaba kaabay mein chamka noor-e-imaan baar chaand.<br />
Dekhiye Allah ke ghar ko lagay ab chaar chaand.</p>
