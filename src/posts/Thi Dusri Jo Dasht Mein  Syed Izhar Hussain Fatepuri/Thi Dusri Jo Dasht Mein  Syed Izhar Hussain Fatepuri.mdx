<p>Thi dusri jo dasht mein daakhil huay Imam.<br/>Thi dusri jo dasht mein daakhil huay Imam.</p>



<p>Aur teesri ko aanay lagi sab sipah-e-shaam.<br/>Chauthi ko shimr ke hui aanay ki dhoom dhaam.<br/>Aur paanchween ko bhar gaya lashkar se ban tamam.<br/>Narga hua chhatti se shah-e-mashraqain par.<br/>Haftum se band ho gaya paani Hussain par.</p>



<p>Thi dusri.</p>
