<p>Cher diya hai zikr-e-Maula mimber Jaanay Haider Jaanay.<br />
Cher diya hai zikr-e-Maula mimber Jaanay Haider Jaanay.<br />
Kufr hai gar ye ishq Ali ka Kaafar Jaane Haider Jaane.</p>
<p>Bint-e-Asad to jaayengi har haal mein kaabay kai andar. (x2)<br />
Dar hogi deewaar kahan se maadar jaanay Haider jaanay.</p>
<p>Ismaeel ye soch rahay thay kyun gardan par haath rakhun.<br />
Naam-e-Ali hai mere labon par khanjar jaanay Haider jaanay.</p>
<p>Fatha likhi hai dast-e-Khuda par ab bhi mere pyaare habeeb. (x2)<br />
Naad-e-Aliyyan keh ke bula lain khyber jaanay Haider jaanay.</p>
<p>Jang-e-Uhad main mera mola shaakh se sar karta hai kalam. (x2)<br />
Taigh falak se aaye na aaye lashkar jaanay Haider jaanay.</p>
<p>Ishq-e-Ali ke daaway daaron Rab ki ibadat laazim hai.<br />
Yeh keh ker mat jaan churao mehshar jaanay Haider jaanay.</p>
<p>Sher-e-Khuda ka pota hai ye keh ker maa ne bhej diya. (x2)<br />
Larta hai ab kaisay run mein Asgher jaanay Haider jaanay.</p>
<p>Shaam mein aur mairaj-e-nabi main aik hi lehja goya tha.<br />
Kis ne rakha hai kis ka parda dukhtar jaanay Haider jaanay.</p>
<p>Mir Hasan aur Sibt-e-Jafar banda-e-Haider hain donoan.<br />
In ke kafan par likhwa dena nokar jaanay Haider jaanay.</p>
<p>Haider Jaanay Haider Jaanay.<br />
Haider Jaanay Haider Jaanay.<br />
Haider Jaanay.</p>
