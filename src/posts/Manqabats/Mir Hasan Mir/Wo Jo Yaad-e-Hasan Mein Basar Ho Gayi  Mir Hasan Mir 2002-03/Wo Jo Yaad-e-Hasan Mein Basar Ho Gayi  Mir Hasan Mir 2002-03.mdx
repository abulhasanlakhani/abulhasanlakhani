<h3>Wo Jo Yaad-e-Hasan Mein</h3>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan mein basar ho gayi (x2).<br/>Bas wohi zindagi mo’tabar ho gayi</p>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan…</p>



<p>Shak parastoa’n ka naam-o-nishaa’n mit gaya (x2).<br/>Shaam honay se pehlay sehar ho gayi.</p>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan…</p>



<p>Aaj kausar ka mafhoom waazeh hua (x2).<br/>Aamad-e-Mustafa kaargar ho gayi.</p>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan…</p>



<p>Innama hal ataa minniyyat Mustafa (x2).<br/>Shakhsiyat khoob se khoob tar ho gayi.</p>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan…</p>



<p>Sulh-e-Shabbar mo’assar hui is tarha’n (x2).<br/>Baat badhti magar mukhtasar ho gayi.</p>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan…</p>



<p>Ye kamaal-e-sha’oor-e-Hasan dekhiye (x2).<br/>Teigh uthi nahi jang sar ho gayi.</p>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan…</p>



<p>Hum Hussaini idhar wo yazeedi udhar (x2).<br/>Khuld is simt dozakh udhar ho gayi.</p>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan…</p>



<p>Mujh pe Ashraf Hasan ka hua ye karam (x2).<br/>Raah-e-Jannat ki mujhko khabar ho gayi.</p>



<p>Wo jo yaad-e-Hasan…</p>
