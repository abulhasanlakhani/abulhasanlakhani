<p>Hussain Bant Rahai Hain Nijaat Lai Jao <br/>Kuch Ansoo’n Kay Aewaz Kainaat Lai Jao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant Rahai Hain Nijaat Lai Jao </p>



<p>Yeh Karbala Hai Yeh Mojiz Numa Ki Basti Hai<br/>Kafan Main Us Ko Lapaito Hayat Lai Jao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant…</p>



<p>Kahain Gay Matmi Halko’n Say Log Mehshar Main<br/>Hamain Hussain Ki Jannat Main Sath Lai Jao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant…</p>



<p>Nahi Karunga Lahad Main Main Intezar e Sahar<br/>Farishto Karbobala Raton Raat Laijao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant…</p>



<p>Qasam Hussain Ki Pursay Main Kam Hi Lagta Hai<br/>Buland Jitna Bhi Matam Main Hath Laijao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant…</p>



<p>Kaha Yeh Khizr Say Abbas Nay Yahan Akar<br/>Hamari Mashq Say Abe Hayaat Lai Jao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant…</p>



<p>Aey Mojo Utho Areezay Ko Sath Lai Jao<br/> Imam Asr Talak Mairi Baat LaiJao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant…</p>



<p>Khuda Utarnay Wala Hai Sadqa Mehdi Ka<br/>Rasoolo’n Ao Naye Mojizat Lai Jao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant…</p>



<p>Kahaingi Fatima Waqt e Zahoor Mehdi Say<br/>Larai Kay Liye Ghazi Ko Sath Lai Jao</p>



<p>Hussain Bant…</p>



<p>Gham e Hussain Main Har Zakhm Mojiza Hai Naeem<br/>Saja Kay Jism Pay Kuch Mojizat Lai Jao</p>
