<p>Suraj ye keh ke deen ka ubhra ghadeer mein.<br />
Takmeel aaj paayega kalma ghadeer mein.</p>
<p>Elaan-e-Jibraeel ye goonja ghadeer mein.<br />
Maula banaaye jaatay hain Maula ghadeer mein.</p>
<p>Bazoo buland kar ke dikhatay rahay Rasool. (x2)<br />
Andhoan ne phir bhi kuch nahi dekha ghadeer mein. (x2)</p>
<p>Sadiyoan ka faasla hai mere un ke darmiyaan. (x2)<br />
Lekin main jaanta hoon ke main tha ghadeer mein.</p>
<p>Quraa’n ki aayatoan ka safar intiha pe tha.<br />
Thi ibtida pe nahj-e-balagah ghadeer mein.</p>
<p>Rasta bhatak gaya tha musafir ghadeer ka.<br />
Hur bas zara si dair se pohncha ghadeer mein.</p>
<p>Ishq-e-Ali mein hota taqayya agar haraam.<br />
Aayat kabhi badalti na lehja ghadeer mein.</p>
<p>Lekin main jaanta hoon ke main tha ghadeer mein.</p>
