<p>**Jo log mae ishq se**</p>
<p><div class="one_half"></p>
<p>Jo log mae ishq se sarshaar rahenge.<br />
Jo log mae ishq se sarshaar rahenge.</p>
<p>Mehshar mein wohi khuld ke haqdaar rahainge.<br />
Jo log mae ishq se sarshar rahainge (x2).</p>
<p>1. Insaaf qayamat mein ye aelaan karay ga.<br />
Jo dushman-e-Shabbir hai dozakh mein rahay ga.<br />
Jannat mein faqad Sheh ke azaadar rahainge.<br />
Jo log mae…</p>
<p>2. Abbas ki aamad hai machay gi wo tabahi.<br />
Aayega nazar phir koi lushkar na sipaahi.<br />
Maidaan mein faqad laashon ke anbaar rahainge.<br />
Jo log mae…</p>
<p>3. Kehtay hain ye taareekh ke mazboot ishaaray.<br />
Pehlu mein paday honay se kya hota hai pyaaray.<br />
Gaddaar to gaddaar hain gaddaar rahainge.<br />
Jo log…</p>
<p>4. Maidaan mein hon mehfil mein hon ya aur kahin hon.<br />
Mimbar pe hon ke takht pe ya khaak nasheen hon.<br />
Sardaar to sardaar hain sardaar rahainge.<br />
Jo log…</p>
<p>5. Ye parcham-e-Ghazi ki ziyaarat nahi kartay.<br />
Ye maatam-e-Shabbir mein uth bhi nahi saktay.<br />
Badkaar tere haath to bekaar rahainge.<br />
Jo log…</p>
<p>6. Na in pe karam hoga na badlay ga muqaddar.<br />
In buugz ke maaron ka to mar jaana hai behtar.<br />
Dunya mein rahainge bhi to bekaar rahainge.<br />
Jo log…</p>
<p>7. Baazu hain kalam in ke koi keh na sakay ga.<br />
Ghaazi ka alam ooncha hai ooncha hi rahay ga.<br />
Abbas alamdaar (x2) Alamdaar rahainge.<br />
Jo log…</p>
<p>8. Kar de tu sar-e-bazm ye aelaan bhi Gohar.<br />
Gairoan ko ijaazat nahi aanay ki yahan par.<br />
Dil mein to meri jaan mere sarkaar rahainge.</p>
<p>Jo log mae ishq se sarshaar rahenge (2).</p>
