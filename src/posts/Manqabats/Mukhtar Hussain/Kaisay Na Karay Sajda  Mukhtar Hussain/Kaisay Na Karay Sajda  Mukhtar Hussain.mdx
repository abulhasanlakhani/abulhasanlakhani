<p>Kaisay na karay sajda, taara dar-e-Zehra par (x2).<br />
Suraj nazar aata hai (x2), zarra dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay sajda, taara dar-e-Zehra par</p>
<p>1. Jo Aal-e-Muhammad ki khairaat se waakif hai (x2).<br />
Wo lekay nahi jaata (x2), kaasa dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay…</p>
<p>2. Is khwab ke aalam se rishta na mera tootay (x2).<br />
Kehta hai koi mujhse (x2), so ja dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay…</p>
<p>3. Hum sab ki ibadat hai kaabay ki taraf sajda (x2).<br />
Kaabay ke ibadat hai, sajda dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay…</p>
<p>4. Us dar pe puhanchnay se pehle chalo is dar par (x2).<br />
Maujood abhi tak hai (x2), Fizza dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay…</p>
<p>5. Is dar ki faqeeri to khuddaar banati hai (x2).<br />
Milta hai khudi ka bhi, tamgha dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay…</p>
<p>6. Kazim ho ya Muslim ho, Abbas ya Asghar ho (x2).<br />
Har baab-e-hawaaij ko, dekha dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay…</p>
<p>7. Mat dhoond mazaaron mein, tu baad-e-kaza us ko (x2).<br />
Milna hai Takallum se, aa ja dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay sajda, taara dar-e-Zehra par.<br />
Suraj nazar aata hai (x2), zarra dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
<p>Kaisay na karay sajda, taara dar-e-Zehra par.</p>
