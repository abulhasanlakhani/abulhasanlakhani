<p>Kahan se laaye gi dunya Hussain ke jaisa (x2).<br />

<p>Kahan se laaye gi dunya Hussain ke jaisa (x2).</p>
<p>1. Ibaadaton ka to jaari hai silsila ab tak.<br />
Namaz ko woh namazi nahi mila ab tak.<br />
Karay jo khaak pe sajda…</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi… (x2)</p>
<p>2. Khuda bhi naaz karay fakhr-e-aalameen banay.<br />
Hai us Rasool ka haq Shah-e-Murasaleen banay.<br />
Hai jis nabi ka nawasa…</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi… (x2)</p>
<p>3. Fazeelatain jo koi Karbala ki poochay ga.<br />
Mujhay yaqeen hai Kaaba jawab ye de ga.<br />
Nahi kisi ka bhi roza…</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi… (x2)</p>
<p>4. Dilair maan rahi hain shuja’atain tum ko.<br />
Salam karti hain Asghar shahadatain tum ko.<br />
Hai cheh mahinay may jazba…</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi… (x2)</p>
<p>5. Shikast kis ki hai maidaan main faisla kar le.<br />
Daleelain chor de aa ja Mubahila kar le.<br />
Hai teray paas to le aa…</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi… (x2)</p>
<p>6. Ye sirf khat nahi tehreer hai muqaddar ki.<br />
Habib tum pe inaayat hui hai Sarwar ki.<br />
Tumhay bulata hai Maula…</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi… (x2)</p>
<p>7. Nusayriyon se kaho chor dain bazid hona.<br />
Qabool kaise karay ga wo lum yalid hona.<br />
Jisay naseeb ho beta…</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi… (x2)</p>
<p>8. Khuda hi janay ke Abbas kya hai shaan teri.<br />
Ye manzilat ye sa’aadat kisay naseeb hui.<br />
Tujhe samajhti hain Zehra…</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi… (x2)</p>
<p>9. Sikha gayi hain zamany ko Sani-e-Zehra.<br />
Yehi aqeeda hai Mukhtar aur Takallum ka.<br />
Kahan hai wird koi Ya Hussain ke jaisa.</p>
<p>Kahan se laaye gi dunya Hussain ke jaisa. (x2)</p>
