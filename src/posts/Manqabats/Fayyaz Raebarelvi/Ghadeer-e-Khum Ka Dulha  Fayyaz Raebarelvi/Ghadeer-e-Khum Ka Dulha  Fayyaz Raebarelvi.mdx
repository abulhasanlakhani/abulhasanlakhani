<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">**Ghadeer-e-Khum ka dulha Abu Talib ka beta**</span></p>
<p>Abu Talib ka beta Abu Talib ka beta (x2).<br />
Fazilat ka meenara Abu Talib ka beta (x2).<br />
Ghadeer-e-Khum ka dulha Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Ali murdoan ko jilaaye Ali suraj paltaaye (x2).<br />
Karishmay dekh ke jis ke zamana dhoka khaaye.<br />
Nuseri samajh na paaye.<br />
Khuda hai ya hai banda Abu Talib ka beta (x2).<br />
Ghadeer-e-khum ka doolha…</p>
<p>Nasab kuch aisa paaya Nabi ne beti dedi (x2).<br />
Shab-e-hijrat jo soya Khuda ne marzi de di (x2).<br />
Sharaf ka arsh-e-moallah Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Jo in se bugz rakhay ga badi mushkil se maray ga (x2).<br />
Yahan bhi haath malay ga wahan bhi haath malay ga (x2).<br />
Hai bakhsish ka darwaza Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Usay kehtay ho kaafir Payambar ka jo chacha hai (x2).<br />
Ae farzi baap ke betoan woh sab kuch dekh raha hai (x2).<br />
Hai ab bhi gaib main betha Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Mera imaan Ali hai meri pehchaan Ali hai (x2).<br />
Zamanay waloan sun lo mera sultan Ali hai (x2).<br />
Shehanshahi ka idara Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Hui jab in ki wiladat butoan ki aayi shamat (x2).<br />
Muqaddar deen ka jaaga mili kaabay ko taharat (x2).<br />
Wiqaar-e-Khana-e-Kaaba Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Mila jab maal-e-ghaneemat ikhatta ho gayi ummat (x2).<br />
Magar jab deen-e-Nabi ko padhi hai khoon ki zaroorat (x2).<br />
Nikal kar saamnay aaya Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Woh nikla khaima-e-haq se bani hashim ka jiyaala (x2).<br />
Batati hai ye jalalat bitha do kitna hi pehra (x2).<br />
Utha le jaayega darya Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Jab ik halka sa tabassum bana shamsheer-e-jalalat (x2)<br />
To phir Fayyaz na jaanay woh hoti kaisi qayamat (x2).<br />
Agar khul kar hans deta Abu Talib ka beta (x2).</p>
<p>Khuda hai ya banda Abu Talib ka beta.</p>
