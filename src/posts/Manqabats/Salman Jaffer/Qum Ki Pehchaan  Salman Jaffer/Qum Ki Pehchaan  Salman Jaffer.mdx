<p>Qum ki pehchaan Masooma-e-Qum. (x2)<br />
Aisi burhaan Masooma-e-Qum.</p>
<p>Bibi Zehra ka inhain naam mila. (x2)<br />
Aisi zeeshan Masooma-e-Qum.</p>
<p>Bibi Zainab ki misl bhai par. (x2)<br />
Dil se qurban Masooma-e-Qum.</p>
<p>Aisay ghar mein hui hain khalq jahaa’n.<br />
Utra Qura’an Masooma-e-Qum.</p>
<p>Jis tarha’n ronak-e-Mashad hain Raza. (x2)<br />
Qum ki hain shaan Masooma-e-Qum.</p>
<p>Wo bulati hain phir se rozay par. (x2)<br />
Tum ko Salman Masooma-e-Qum.</p>
<p>Qum ki pehchaan Masooma-e-Qum.</p>
