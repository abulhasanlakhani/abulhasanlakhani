<p>Aa gayin Fatima, Aa gayin Fatima (x2)<br />
Aa gayin Fatima, Aa gayin Fatima (x2)</p>
<p>Jibra’eel-e-Ameen de rahay hain sada.<br />
Aa gayin… (x2).<br />
Aaj khush hain bohat ashraf-ul-Ambiya (SAWW).<br />
Aa gayin… (x2).</p>
<p>1. (Arsh se farsh tak noor ka hai samaa.<br />
Aayin aalam mein giyaara imamoan ki maa (x2)).<br />
Duur aalam ka saara andhera hua.<br />
Aa gayin… (x2)</p>
<p>2. (Mustafa aur Hasnain aur Murtaza.<br />
Hukm-e-Khaaliq se jab ho gaye aik jaan (x2)).<br />
Phir durodon ki baarish mein zair-e-kisa.<br />
Aa gayin… (x2)</p>
<p>3. (Jis ke dar se malak rizq paate rahay.<br />
Aur ban ban ke khayyaat aate rahay (x2)).<br />
Bheek leni hai ja jaldi kashkol la.<br />
Aa gayin… (x2)</p>
<p>4. (Main gunahgar kya meri auqaat kya.<br />
Aik aansu ke badlay sila yeh mila (x2)).<br />
Khuld se khud mere haq mein karnay dua.<br />
Aa gayin… (x2)</p>
<p>5. (Main Hasan mushkilon mein gira jab kabhi.<br />
Main ne darbaar-e-Zahra mein faryaad ki (x2)).<br />
Khatm honay na paayi thi meri dua.<br />
Aa gayin… (x2)</p>
