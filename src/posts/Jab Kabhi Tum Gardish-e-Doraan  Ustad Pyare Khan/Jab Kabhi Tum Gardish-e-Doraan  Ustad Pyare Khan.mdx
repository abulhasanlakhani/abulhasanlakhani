<p>Jab kabhi tum gardish-e-doraan se ghabraya karo.<br />
Parcham-e-Abbas ke saaye main aa jaaya karo.</p>
<p>Jab kabhi paida ho dil mein khahish-e-khuld-e-bareen (x2).<br />
Majlis-e-Shabbir mein aaya karo jaaya karo (x2).</p>
<p>Jab kabhi tum…</p>
<p>Khud ba khud fiqr-o-amal ki tarbiyat ho jaayegi (x2).<br />
Apnay bachoan se alam Ghazi ka uthwaya karo (x2).</p>
<p>Jab kabhi tum…</p>
<p>Rizq mein wus’at milay gi barkatain badh jayaingi (x2).<br />
Haazri-e-Hazrat-e-Abbas karwaya karo (x2).</p>
<p>Jab kabhi tum…</p>
<p>Habs ke mahol mein jab bhi ghutan mehsoon ho (x2).<br />
Khushu-e-zikr-e-Ali se ghar ko mehkaya karo (x2).</p>
<p>Aayay jab madd-e-muqabil waqt ka koi yazeed (x2).<br />
Ya Hussain ibn-e-Ali keh keh ke thukraya karo (x2).</p>
<p>Jab kabhi tum gardish-e-doraan se ghabraya karo.</p>
