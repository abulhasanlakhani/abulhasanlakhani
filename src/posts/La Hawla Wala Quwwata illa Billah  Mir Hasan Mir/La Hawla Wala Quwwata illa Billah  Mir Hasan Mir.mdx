<p>La Hawla Wala Quwwata illa Billah. (x4)<br />
Main aur karoon (x2) dushman-e-Haider ki sana.<br />
La Hawla Wala Quwwata illa Billah. (x3)</p>
<p>Dawlat ke liye phir gaye haq se yaksar. (x2)<br />
Tum dushman-e-Shabbir ho laanat tum par.<br />
Hurr shaam ke lashkar se ye keh kar nikla.</p>
<p>La Hawla Wala. (x2)</p>
<p>Haider ki wilaayat pe jo shak karta hai. (x2)<br />
Imaan hai mera ke woh jab marta hai.<br />
Padhti hai ajal dekh ke us ka chehra.</p>
<p>La Hawla Wala. (x2)</p>
<p>Aal-e-abu sufiyaan se humdardi hai. (x2)<br />
Aye dunya adawat ki bhi hadh hoti hai.<br />
Kaafir Abu Taalib hai to Islam hai kya.</p>
<p>La Hawla Wala.  (x2)</p>
<p>Tu meri kahi baat pe shak karta hai. (x2)<br />
Allah ke banday ko Khuda kehta hai.<br />
Ye keh ke. (x2) nusairi ko Ali ne maara.</p>
<p>La Hawla Wala  (x2)</p>
<p>Imaan-o-Aqeede ki durasti kar le. (x2)<br />
Hai shakl buri baat to achi kar le.<br />
Kehta hai Muhammad ko tu apne jaisa.</p>
<p>La Hawla Wala.  (x2)</p>
<p>Takraane lagi. (x3) Kufay se faujain ja ke.<br />
Sheh ne kiye is tarhan se satra hamlay.<br />
Har hamlay pe kehta raha Ibn-e-Zehra.</p>
<p>La Hawla Wala Quwwata illa Billah.  (x2)</p>
<p>Mat ye kaho iblees ke jaise hongay. (x2)<br />
Socho to zara log woh kaisay hongay.<br />
Iblees jinhain dekh ke padhta hoga.</p>
<p>La Hawla Wala Quwwata illa Billah.  (x2)</p>
<p>Bachna ae Takallum ye maraz hai moozi. (x2)<br />
Hain saamnay aankhoan ke misaalain kitni.<br />
Shaitan bana deta hai sho’hrat ka nasha.</p>
<p>La Hawla Wala Quwwata illa Billah.  (x2)</p>
