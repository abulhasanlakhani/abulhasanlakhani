<p>Jab rida sar se chini main sada deti rahi.<br />
Tu na aaya Ghazi.</p>
<p>Aal-e-Imran kahan aur zindan kahan.<br />
Ye behan qaid hui. Tu na aaya Ghazi…</p>
<p>Jab rida sar…</p>
<p>1. Hum ko paani na milay teri khusbu to rahay.<br />
Tere baazu na katain chahay mashkeeza chiday.<br />
Ye magar ho na saka tere baazu hain juda.<br />
Mujh pe hai tashnalabi. Tu na…</p>
<p>Jab rida sar…</p>
<p>2. Dhoop mein tu tha shajar tujh se abaad tha ghar.<br />
Hai barehna mera sar kya nahi tujh ko khabar.<br />
Aye alamdar-e-wafa is behan ko ba Khuda.<br />
Tujh se dharas thi badi. Tu na…</p>
<p>Jab rida sar…</p>
<p>3. Aa gayi shaam-e-alam lut gaye ahl-e-haram.<br />
Rayt par jalti huvi ho gaya thanda alam.<br />
Pursa denay ke liye mujh se milnay ke liye.<br />
Aa gayay baba Ali tu na…</p>
<p>Jab rida sar…</p>
<p>4. Kya kahoon sher meray be rida hum ko liye.<br />
Ye musalmaan saaray shehr dar shehr gayay.<br />
Khilqat-e-koofa kabhi khilqat-e-shaam kabhi.<br />
Baar ha hum pe hasi tu na…</p>
<p>Jab rida sar…</p>
<p>5. Kitni be bas thi behan aye shehanshah-e-wafa.<br />
Naam le le ke mera jab ye zaalim ne kaha.<br />
Naaz tha jis pe tujhay ab bulao na usay.<br />
Aur main roti rahi tu na…</p>
<p>Jab rida sar…</p>
<p>6. Qaid khanay mein qaza jab Sakina ko mili.<br />
Dekay kurtay ka kafan bachi dafnaayi gayi.<br />
Us ghadi naam tera surat-e-naad-e-Ali.<br />
Dukh mein dohrati rahi tu na…</p>
<p>Jab rida sar…</p>
<p>7. Roya Rehan qalam kar ke ye baat raqam.<br />
Khoon mein doob gaya meray Ghazi ka alam.<br />
Zakhmi Zainab ka jigar khoon fishaan Shah ka sar.<br />
Aayay Khaimon mein shaqi tu na aaya Ghazi.</p>
<p>Jab rida sar…</p>
