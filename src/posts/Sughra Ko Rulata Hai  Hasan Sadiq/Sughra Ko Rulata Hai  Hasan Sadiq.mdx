<p>Sughra ko rulata hai ye chaand muharram ka.<br />
Sughra ko rulata hai ye chaand muharram ka (x2).<br />
Jab saamnay aata hai (x2). Ye chaand muharram ka.<br />
Sughra ko rulata hai…</p>
<p>1. Hum surat-e-Zehra se bhai ke bichadnay ki.<br />
Khatoon-e-qayamat ke ghar baar ujadnay ki.<br />
Ghar baar ujadnay ki.<br />
Rudaad sunata hai. Ye chaand muharram ka.<br />
Sughra ko… (x2).</p>
<p>2. Kaata hai gala logoan seh roz ke pyaase ka.<br />
Pamaal hua laasha Ahmed ke nawasay ka.<br />
Ahmed ke nawasay ka.<br />
Har ek ko batata hai. Ye chaand muharram ka.<br />
Sughra ko… (x2)</p>
<p>3. Tasveer dikhata hai Ahmed ki jawani ki.<br />
Har shakhs ko dunya mein karbal ki kahani ki.<br />
Karbal ki kahani ki.<br />
Tafseer batata hai. Ye chaand muharram ka.<br />
Sughra ko… (x2).</p>
<p>4. Karbal ki ziyarat ko jo aankh tarasti thi.<br />
Shabbir tere gham mein jo aankh barasti thi.<br />
Jo aankh barasti thi.<br />
Us ankh ko bhaata hai. Ye chaand muharram ka.<br />
Sughra ko… (x2).</p>
<p>5. Imran ko Shabbar ko khatoon-e-qayamat ko.<br />
Haider ki kasam mazhar Sarkar-e-Risaalat ko.<br />
Sarkar-e-Risaalat ko.<br />
Turbat mein rulata hai. Ye chaand muharram ka.<br />
Sughra ko… (x2).</p>
<p>Jab saamnay aata hai (x2). Ye chaand muharram ka.<br />
Sughra ko… (x2).</p>
