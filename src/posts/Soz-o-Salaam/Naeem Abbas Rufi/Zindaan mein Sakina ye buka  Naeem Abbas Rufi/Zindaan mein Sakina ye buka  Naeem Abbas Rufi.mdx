<p><span class="a">Zindaa’n mein Sakina ye buka karti thi ro kar (x2).<br />
</span><span class="a">Dum ghut-ta hai amma mera. Ab yaa se chalo ghar.</span></p>
<p>Zindaa’n mein Sakina ye buka…</p>
<p><span class="a">1. Kya andhera hai sujhayi nahi deta kuch bhi (x2).<br />
</span><span class="a">Haath rakhta nahi amma mere sar par koi.<br />
</span><span class="a">Aur chund roz mein mar jaoongi main ghut ghut kar.</span></p>
<p>Zindaa’n mein…</p>
<p><span class="a">2. Khaak pe soti hoon baba ka nahi hai seena (x2).<br />
</span><span class="a">Kitna dushwaar hai is dard-o-alam mein jeena.<br />
</span><span class="a">Raat din roti hu kar de koi Baba ko khabar.</span></p>
<p>Zindaa’n mein…</p>
<p><span class="a">3. Khoon bhara kurta badal do mera pyari Amma (x2).<br />
</span><span class="a">Apni godi mein sula lo mujhe waari Amma.<br />
</span><span class="a">Sonay deta nahi ik pal mujhay ye hijr-e-padar.</span></p>
<p>Zindaa’n mein…</p>
<p><span class="a">4. Tum to kehti thi rihaayi ki ghadi aa pohnchi (x2).<br />
</span><span class="a">Aisa lagta hai judaayi ki ghadi aa pohnchi.<br />
Khwaab mein aayay thay mere abhi dada Haider.<br />
</span></p>
<p>Zindaa’n mein…</p>
<p><span class="a">5. Neel rukhsar ke kaanoan ka lahoo taaza hai (x2).<br />
</span><span class="a">Kuch mere dard ka Amma tumhain andaaza hai.<br />
</span><span class="a">Ghudhkiyan shimr jo deta hai to phat-ta hai jigar.</span></p>
<p><span class="a">Zindaa’n mein…</span></p>
<p><span class="a">6. Neend aati na thi Rehan jisay wo bachhi (x2).<br />
</span><span class="a">So gayi sham ke zindaan mein ye kehti hui.<br />
</span><span class="a">Chayn se souungi jaaongi jo zindaan se ghar.</span></p>
<p>Zindaa’n mein Sakina ye buka karti thi ro kar (x2).</p>
