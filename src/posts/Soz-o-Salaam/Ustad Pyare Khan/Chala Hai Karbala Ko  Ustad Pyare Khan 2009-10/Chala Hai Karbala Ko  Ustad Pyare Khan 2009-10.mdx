<p>Chala hai karbala ko kaarwan… aahista aahista (x2)<br/>Alam ki chhaa rahi hai badriyaan… aahista aahista.<br/>Chala hai karbala…</p>



<p>1- Ali Akbar jawaani ki kasam mur kar zara dekho (x2).<br/>Chali aati hai peechay peechay maa… aahista aahista (x2).<br/>Chala hai karbala ko karwaan… aahista aahista.</p>



<p>2- Pidar ki kuwwat-e-bardasht ka tha dhiaan Akbar ko (x2).<br/>Badi mushkil se li angrayiaan… aahista aahista (x2).</p>



<p>3- Badan sab choor tha zakhmoan se Qasim ka dum-e-urdan.<br/>Badi mushkil se li thi hichkiyaan… aahista aahista.</p>



<p>4- Kinaaray nehar ke Abbas aaye to salaami ko (x2).<br/>Nikal aayi thi saari machliaan… aahista aahista.</p>



<p>Chala hai karbala.</p>
