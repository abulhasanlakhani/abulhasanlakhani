<h3>Gham-e-Hussain Ka Maksad</h3>



<p>Gham-e-Hussain ka maksad badal nahi sakta (x2).<br/>Khayaam jal gaye paigaam jal nahi sakta.<br/>Gham-e-Hussain ka.</p>



<p>1. Ye baat bhool gaye dushman-e-Abu Talib (x2).<br/>Rasool kufr ke daaman mein pal nahi sakta.</p>



<p>2. Hussain zinda hai jab tak zamana baaki hai (x2).<br/>Yazeediat ka shajar phool phal nahi sakta.</p>



<p>3. Wo jis ki aankh mein aansoo gham-e-Hussain ke hain.<br/>Yaqeen hai aatish-e-dozakh mein jal nahi sakta.</p>



<p>4. Na Sheh ko subh-e-alam se dara shab-e-aashoor (x2).<br/>Hussain keh dain to suraj nikal nahi sakta.</p>



<p>5. Nikal to aayega naawuk gulu-e-Asghar se (x2).<br/>Magar Rubab ke dil se nikal nahi sakta.</p>
