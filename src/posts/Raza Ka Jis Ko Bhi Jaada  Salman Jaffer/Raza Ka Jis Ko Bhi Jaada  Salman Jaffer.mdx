<p>Raza ka jis ko bhi jaada naseeb hota hai. (x2)<br />
Usay talab se ziyada naseeb hota hai.<br />
Raza ka jis ko bhi…</p>
<p>1- Dua karo woh bulaayain phir apnay rozay par. (x2)<br />
Wahin se ye bhi iraada naseeb hota hai.</p>
<p>2- Milay jo hubb-e-Ali wus’at-e-nigaah ke saath. (x2)<br />
To us ko dil bhi kushaada naseeb hota hai.</p>
<p>3- Sada jamaat-e-ahl-e-wila hai rozay par. (x2)<br />
Sharaf kahan ye furaada naseeb hota hai.</p>
<p>4- Ye mojiza hai ke sab ko pohanch ke mash’had mein. (x2)<br />
Qaraar hadh se ziyada naseeb hota hai.</p>
<p>Raza ka jis ko bhi jaada naseeb hota hai.</p>
