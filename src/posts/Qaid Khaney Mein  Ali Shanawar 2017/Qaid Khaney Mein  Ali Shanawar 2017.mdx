<p>**Qaid Khaney Mein**</p>
<p>Baba. Baba. Baba.<br />
Sun rahay ho sada baba jaan (x2).<br />
Baba jaan…</p>
<p>Hum kahan aa gaye ghar kahan reh gaya (x2).<br />
Qaid khaney mein noha Sakina ka tha (x2).<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye ghar kahan reh gaya (x2).</p>
<p>1. Yaad aata hai guzra zamaana mujhe.<br />
Le ke baahoan mein jhoola jhulana mujhe.<br />
Main bulaati rahi aur na aaye chacha.<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye… (x2).<br />
Qaid khaney mein noha Sakina ka tha.<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye… (x2).</p>
<p>2. Yaad aata hai pyaara Madina mujhe.<br />
Phool jaisa wo baba ka seena mujhe.<br />
Wo zamana koi phir dikha de mujhe.<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye… (x2).<br />
Qaid khaney…<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye… (x2).</p>
<p>3. Bhai Abid ne chalna sikhaya mujhe.<br />
Apnay haathon se khana khilaya mujhe.<br />
Ab usi ke liye hai dawa na ghiza.<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye… (x2).<br />
Qaid khaney…<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye… (x2).</p>
<p>4. Hai baba ko dekhay zamana hua.<br />
Un ke seenay pe soye zamana hua.<br />
Na tumhara pata na hamara pata.<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye… (x2).<br />
Qaid khaney…<br />
Hum kahan aa gaye… (x2).</p>
<p>Sun rahay ho sada Baba jaan (x2)</p>
