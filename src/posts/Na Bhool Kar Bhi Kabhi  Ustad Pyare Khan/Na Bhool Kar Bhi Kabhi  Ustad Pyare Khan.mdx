<p>Na bhool kar bhi kabhi tum kisi ki baat karo (x2).<br />
Ali ka zikr karo bas Ali ki baat karo (x2).<br />
Na bhool kar bhi…</p>
<p>1. Kisi ka nafs kisi ka wasi kisi ka Khuda (x2).<br />
Ye sab sifaat ho jis mein usi ki baat karo.</p>
<p>2. Ali ke naksh-e-kadam ka main zikr karta hoon.<br />
Tum us ke saath hi dosh-e-Nabi ki baat karo.</p>
<p>3. Ye zikr zikr-e-Nabi hai yehi hai zikr-e-Khuda.<br />
Ali ke hotay huay kyun kisi ki baat karo.</p>
<p>4. Lehad mein meri andhera ho gair mumkin hai.<br />
Jahan Ali ho wahan roshni ki baat karo.<br />
Ali ka zikr karo bas Ali ki baat karo.<br />
Na bhool kar bhi…</p>
<p>5. Azal se saaqi-e-kausar se hoon main waabasta.<br />
Na meray aagay kisi tishnigi ki baat karo.</p>
<p>6. Hai waqt naza ka baadi pa aayay hain Mola.<br />
Kahan ki maut bas ab zindagi ki baat karo.</p>
<p>7. Sar-e-niyaaz jhuka do Ali chaukhat par.<br />
Guroor-o-naaz se phir bandagi ki baat karo.</p>
<p>8. Samajh mein aana sakay ga kabhi Ali ka maqaam.<br />
Na is tarhan kabhi deewangi ki baat karo.</p>
<p>9. Farishton kaisa sawal-o-jawab turbat mein (x2).<br />
Ab aa gaye ho to betho Ali ki baat karo.</p>
