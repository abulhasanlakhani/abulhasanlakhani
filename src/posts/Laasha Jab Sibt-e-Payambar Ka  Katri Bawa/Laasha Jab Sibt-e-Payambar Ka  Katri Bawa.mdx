<p>Laasha jab Sibt-e-Payambar ka uthaaya hoga (x2).<br />
Khuld mein chayn Muhammad ko na aaya hoga (x2).<br />
Laasha jab sibt-e-Payambar ka uthaaya hoga (x2).</p>
<p>1. Sar khulay lehad se tab Fatima nikli hogi (x2).<br />
Zehr jodah ne jo Shabbar ko pilaya hoga (x2).<br />
Laasha jab sibt-e-Payambar… (x2)</p>
<p>2. Kabr mein Hasan to us waqt hi utray hongay (x2).<br />
Maa ne jab daaman-e-tatheer bichaaya hoga (x2).<br />
Laasha jab sibt-e-Payambar… (x2)</p>
<p>3. Daastan Hasan ne apni jo sunaayi hogi (x2).<br />
Maa ne ro ro ke kalejay se lagaaya hoga (x2).<br />
Laasha jab sibt-e-Payambar… (x2)</p>
<p>4. Dil ke sab Hasan ne armaan nikaalay hongay (x2).<br />
Sehra Qasim ko khayaaloan mein lagaaya hoga (x2).<br />
Laasha jab sibt-e-Payambar… (x2)</p>
<p>5. Jo ke maadar ke janaazay pe na ghar se nikli (x2).<br />
Kaisay beemaar unhain shaam mein laaya hoga (x2).<br />
Laasha jab sibt-e-Payambar… (x2)</p>
