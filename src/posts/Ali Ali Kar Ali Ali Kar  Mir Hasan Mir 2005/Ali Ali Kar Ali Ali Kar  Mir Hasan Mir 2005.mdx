<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">**Title: Ali Ali kar Ali Ali kar**</span></p>
<p>Shah-e-Mardaa’n Sher-e-Yazdaa’n Quwwat-e-Parwardigaar.<br />
La Fata Illa’h Ali La Saif Illah Zulfiqar.</p>
<p>Zameer-e-Insaa’n ka faisla hai Ali Ali kar Ali Ali Kar.<br />
Ali Ali kar Ali Ali kar. (x2)<br />
Khuda ne Adam se keh diya hai.<br />
Ali Ali kar Ali Ali kar.(x2)</p>
<p>Ali shifa’at ka harf-e-aakhir Ali hai imaan ka tarazu.<br />
Isi ke Shahbaz bhi sawali, gada hain is ke Farid-o-Baahu (x2).<br />
Bhitayi ne bhi yehi kaha hai.<br />
Ali Ali Kar…(x2)</p>
<p>Hamain to ishq-o-wafa ki daulat Ali ke dar se ata hui hai.<br />
Ali wali se hasad aye waayiz, hamaray maslaq main khudkushi hai (x2).<br />
Tujhe payambar ka waasta hai.<br />
Ali Ali kar…(x2)</p>
<p>Ali ka ghar hai woh ghar musalma’n jo aaqibat ko sanwaarta hai.<br />
Zameen kya hai ye aasmaa’n bhi, usi ka sadqa utaarta hai (x2).<br />
Yehi waseela nijaat ka hai.<br />
Ali Ali kar…(x2)</p>
<p>Munafiqat ka hai ye iraada halal sheh ko haraam kar de.<br />
Ye gham aqeeday ki zindagi hai, bas is ka kissa tamam kar de (x2).<br />
Yaqeen ka dum nikal raha hai.<br />
Ya Ali Ya Ali, Ya Ali Ya Ali. (x2)</p>
<p>Zameer-e-Insaa’n ka faisla hai, Ali Ali kar (x2).</p>
<p>Munafiqat ki andheri raahoan pe chal paday hain zamanay walay.<br />
Ye waqt-e-mohlat mila hai tujhko, Hussainiat ka alam utha le (x2).<br />
Hamara tujhko ye mashwara hai.<br />
Ali Ali kar…(x2)</p>
<p>Mohib ke wijdaan ki sada hai Hussainiat ka shi’aar rakhna.<br />
Ali ke ghar se agar hai nisbat, ye farsh-e-gham barqarar rakhna (x2).<br />
Mohib zamana badal raha hai.<br />
Ali Ali kar Ali Ali Kar</p>
<p>Ya Ali Ya Ali, Ya Ali Ya Ali. (x2)</p>
