<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">**Siskiyan Khaimo Se**</span></p>
<p>Hai Sakina Hai Sakina<br />
Na ro Sakina</p>
<p>Siskiyan khaimo se kis ki aa rahi hai aye Hussain (x2).<br />
Kya Sakina ko behan behla rahi hai aye Hussain.</p>
<p>1. Aayainge Abbas paani le ke darya se abhi (x2).<br />
Bint-e-Haider sab ko ye batla rahi hain aye Hussain (x2).</p>
<p>2. Qafila salaar Abid sar khulay hain bibiyaan (x2).<br />
Karbala se su-e-Zindan ja rahi hain aye Hussain (x2).</p>
<p>3. Kha ke Asghar teer ran mein mar gaye lekin Rubab (x2).<br />
Gom khayalon mein usay behla rahay hain aye Hussain (x2).</p>
<p>4. Le ke kurta khoo bhara Asghar ka Zainab saath mein (x2).<br />
Qabr pe nana ki roti ja rahi hai aye Hussain (x2).</p>
<p>5. Ho gaye pamaal Qasim ran mein Akbar so gaye (x2).<br />
Umm-e-Laila yun pachaade kha rahi hain aye Hussain (x2).</p>
<p>6. Be kafan laasho ko ran mein kaun ab dafnaayega (x2).<br />
Rassiyan Zainab ke baandhi ja rahi hain aye Hussain (x2).</p>
<p>7. Ik rasand baara galay hain tauq hai beemar hai.<br />
Bediyaan pairon se lipti ja rahi hain aye Hussain (x2).</p>
<p>8. Lo Hasan baali Sakina dasht ki tanhayi mein (x2).<br />
Ro rahi hai aur kehti ja rahi hai aye Hussain (x2).</p>
<p>Siskiyan khaimo se kis ki aa rahi hai aye Hussain.</p>
