<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">**Aye Kul Ke Madadgaar**</span></p>
<p>Haaye Hussaina haaye Hussaina.<br />
Aye Haaye Sakina aye haaye Sakina.</p>
<p>Ye rukhsat-e-aakhir hai pukaray Shah-e-waala.<br />
Aye Zainab-o-Kulsum Rabab aur Ruqayya.<br />
Tum sab pe salaam aakhri Allah ko Saupa.<br />
Lipti hui kadmo se ye kehti thi Sakina.</p>
<p>Aye kul ke madadgaar madad karnay ko aao.<br />
Ya Fatima Zehra mere baaba ko bachao (x2).</p>
<p>1. Baba na kaho baba. Tum saath raho baba.<br />
Kuch hum se suno baba. Aagay na barho baba.<br />
Aaram karo baba. Baraye dil-e-dukhtar (x2).<br />
Khaimay mein chalo baba.</p>
<p>Aye kul ke…</p>
<p>2. Juyam ba kuja baba. Dar dasht tu ra baba.<br />
Nazdam to biya baba. Yaad az ee wafa baba.<br />
Lutf-e-be-numa baba. Baraye dil-e-dukhtar (x2).<br />
Aye baba ma ru baba.</p>
<p>Aye kul ke…</p>
<p>3. Main jaan gayi baba. Pehchaan gayi baba.<br />
Kurbaan gayi baba. Talwaaro mein jao ge.<br />
Phir tum bhi na aaogay. Baraye dil-e-dukhtar (x2).<br />
Tum hum ko na tadpaana.</p>
<p>Aye kul ke…</p>
<p>4. Aaya dukhtar-e-nees tam. Yakta gohar-e-nees tam.<br />
Noor-e-nazar-e-nistam. Ya humsafar-e-neestam.<br />
Gar bar gardi man neestam. Baraye dil-e-dukhtar (x2).<br />
Bar gard-o-biya baba.</p>
<p>Aye kul ke…</p>
<p>5. Baba aye mere baba. Laazim hai agar Jaana.<br />
Ye hum se karo waada. Jab shab ka ho sannaata.<br />
Aur meri sada sunna. Baraye dil-e-dukhtar (x2).<br />
Tum laut ke aa jaana.</p>
<p>Aye kul ke…</p>
<p>6. Baba ye mani baba. Laazim shud-e-gar raftan.<br />
Ee waada ye ma baa shad. Dar taariki ye shab ha.<br />
Bishnu chu sadaa-e-man. Baraye dil-e-dukhtar (x2).<br />
Baaz aayi baraaye man.</p>
<p>Aye kul ke…</p>
<p>7. Sheh bolay Sakina jaan. Saw jaan se main kurbaan.<br />
Is tarha na ho girya. Ek waada nibhana hai.<br />
Ek qarz puraana hai. Baraye dil-e-dukhtar.<br />
Baraye dil-e-baba. Tum aaj dua karna.</p>
<p>Aye kul ke…</p>
<p>8. Pursa hai pa-e-Maula. Rehan-o-Ali Jee ka.<br />
Kartay thay haram noha. Sarwar ne kaha beti.<br />
Saa at hai judaai ki. Baraye dil-e-dukhtar (x2).<br />
Baraye dil-e-baba. Maqtal na chali aana.</p>
