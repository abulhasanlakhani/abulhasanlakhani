<p>Qaid Khanay Say Sakina Ki Ye Aati Thi Sada</p>
<p>Haaye Sakina (x3)</p>
<p>Haaye Haaye.<br />
Samajh ke Zehra sataya gaya Sakina ko.<br />
Kadam kadam pe rulaya gaya Sakina ko.<br />
Woh zakhm aaj bhi taaza hain.<br />
Pusht-e-naaka se gir rahi hai.<br />
Giraya gaya Sakina ko.<br />
Haaye Haaye.</p>
<p>Qaid khanay se Sakina ki ye aati thi sada.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.</p>
<p>Mujh ko chaltay huay naakay se giraya baba.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.</p>
<p>Ho gayi chaar baras mein hi Sakina Zehra.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.</p>
<p>Qaid Khanay Say Sakina Ki Ye Aati Thi Sada<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.</p>
<p>1. Woh bhi dada se chupaati rahi apna pehlu.<br />
Mujhse bhi dekhay na jayainge Ali ke Aansu (x2).<br />
Jeetay ji meray mera zakhm na dekhain bhayya.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.<br />
Qaid Khanay Say Sakina…</p>
<p>2. Kaisay aayi hoon main baazaar se ho kar baba.<br />
Mujhko maaray gaye is zakhm pe patthar baba (x2).<br />
Ro ke main kehti rahi phir bhi kisi ne na suna.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.<br />
Qaid Khanay Say…</p>
<p>3. Ab samajh sakti hoon kis tarhan woh soti hongi.<br />
Haath pehlu pe rakhay kaisay tadapti hongi.<br />
Karwatain dard mujhe bhi nahi lenay deta.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.<br />
Qaid Khanay Say…</p>
<p>4. Hu-bahu Fatima Zehra se zayeefi chhaayi.<br />
Jhurriyaan chehray pe baalon mein safaydee aayi (x2).<br />
Hai ik kami baaki thi woh bhi hui poori baba.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.<br />
Qaid Khanay Say…</p>
<p>5. Saath suraj ke ye dil doob raha hota hai.<br />
Amma kehti hai yehi waqt-e-dua hota hai (x2).<br />
Roz karti hoon sar-e-shaam main marnay ki dua.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.<br />
Qaid Khanay Say…</p>
<p>6. Ja ke jannat mein bhi aaram nahi aayega.<br />
Kya ye armaan mera dil mein hi reh jaayega.<br />
Kaisay godi mein uthaongi main Asghar ko bhala.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.<br />
Qaid Khanay Say…</p>
<p>7. Aap ka sar jalay daaman mein uthanay ke liye.<br />
Haath darbaar mein mushkil se uthaaye main ne (x2).<br />
Hai tum ko us waqt bhi main ne na bataya baba.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.<br />
Qaid Khanay Say…</p>
<p>8. Gir padi khaak pe namoos-e-Nabi peet ke sar.<br />
Qaid khanay main hui aur qayamat Akbar.<br />
Le ke sar baap ka bachi ne jo ro ro ke kaha.<br />
Pasliyaan toot gayi meri bhi daadi ki tarhan.<br />
Qaid Khanay Say…</p>
