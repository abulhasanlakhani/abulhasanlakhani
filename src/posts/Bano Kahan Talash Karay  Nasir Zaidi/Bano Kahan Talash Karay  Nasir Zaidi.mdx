<p>Bano kahan talash karay apnay laal ko (x2)<br />
Jungle mein maar daala hai Zehra ki aal ko.<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
<p>1. Zainab ke saath roti hain sab mil ke bibiaan (x2).<br />
Jab dekhti thi roz woh waqt-e-zawaal ko.<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
<p>2. Shabbir dil ko thaam ke ghash kha ke gir paday (x2).<br />
Dekha jo khoon mein gark Nabi ke jamaal ko…<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
<p>3. Shabbir mere pass hai sadqay ko bas yehi (x2).<br />
Jhoolay se laa ke de diya Bano ne laal ko.<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
<p>4. Shabbir laa rahay hain maidaan se dekhiye (x2).<br />
Apne zayeef kaandhon pa athara saal ko.<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
<p>5. Bazaar-e-Shaam mein wo aseeron ka kaafila (x2).<br />
Abid na bhool paaye kabhi is khayaal ko.<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
<p>6. Aisa to zulm-o-jaur kisi par hua nahin (x2).<br />
Barbaad jis tarhan kiya Zehra ki aal ko.<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
<p>7. Laila duaayein karti rahi baad-e-karbala (x2).<br />
Ya rab juda na karna kisi maa se laal ko.<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
<p>8. Shabbir apnay rozay pe bulwayainge zaroor (x2).<br />
Tauqeer tu nikal de dil se malaal ko.<br />
Bano kahan talash…</p>
