<p style="font-size:18px">**Na alam na chacha**</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na chacha na shah-e-karbala shaam ho gayi.<br/>Jo bhi ghar se gaya wo na phir aa saka shaam ho gayi.<br/>Na alam na… (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Raah takti rahi shiddat-e-pyaas mein.<br/>Kat gaya din isi aas hee aas mein.<br/>Koi aaya nahi jaane kya ho gaya. <br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na…  (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Pani lenay gayay deyr kyun ho gayi.<br/>Din dhala aye chacha shaam-e-gham aa gayi.<br/>Khushk hai aye chacha mera ab tak gala. <br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na…  (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kis ko awaaz doo’n hoo ke maidaan mein.<br/>Kaun hai jo sunay is bayaabaan mein.<br/>De rahi hoo’n tumhain jaanay kab se sada. <br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na…  (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Laut aao ke paani nahi chahiye.<br/>Khao mujh par taras ab Khuda ke liye.<br/>Mashk khaali hi le aao ab to chacha.<br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na…  (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Jaakay maidaa’n mein baba bhi aaye nahi.<br/>Ab to jhoolay mein Asghar bhi haaye nahi.<br/>Khaima jalta hai yu’n jaisay koi diyaa.<br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na…  (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Ro rahi hai phupphi chin gayi hai ridaa.<br/>Maartay hain tamaachay mujhe ashkiyaa.<br/>Khaima jalta hai yu’n jaisay koi diyaa.<br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na…  (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Baazuoa’n mein rasand baandhnay aa gayay.<br/>Khainch ke le gaye durr mere kaan se.<br/>Ho gaya khoo’n se tar saara kurta mera.<br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na…  (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Lo chacha su-e-zindaa’n chali ghamzada.<br/>Alvida alvida  aye qateel-e-jafa.<br/>Lijiye aap se ho rahi hoon juda.<br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na…  (x2) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Noha Rehaan majlis mein sun ke tera.<br/>Chashmay giryaa’n ko ye hosh kab hi raha.<br/>Kab sehar ho gayi aur kab din dhala.<br/>Shaam ho gayi.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Na alam na chacha na shah-e-karbala shaam ho gayi (x2).</p>
