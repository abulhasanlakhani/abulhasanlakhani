<p>Bay gunah mara gaya sibt-e-Rasool-e-do sara. Wa Hasan-e-sabz qaba (x4)<br />
Kalma goyoan ne ye kya khoob kiya wada wafa. Wa Hasan-e-sabz qaba (x2)</p>
<p>Bay gunah mara gaya…</p>
<p>1. Bhai ki laash pe ro ro ke ye Zainab ne kaha.<br />
Aarzu thi bhayya (x2).<br />
Kaash Abdullah-o-Qasim ko banatay dulha.<br />
Wa Hasan-e-sabz qaba.</p>
<p>Bay gunah mara gaya… (x2)</p>
<p>2. Khoon ugaltay huway bhai ko behan ne dekha.<br />
Aur tadap kar ye kaha (x2).<br />
Teri gurbat pe aye bhai teri Zainab ho fida.<br />
Wa Hasan-e-sabz qaba.</p>
<p>Bay gunah… (x2)</p>
<p>3. Maa ke gham se abhi azaad kahan thi Zainab.<br />
Ho gaya kaisa gazab (x2).<br />
Baap zinda na raha bhai su-e-khuld chala.<br />
Wa Hasan-e-sabz qaba.</p>
<p>Bay gunah… (x2)</p>
<p>4. Ker diya zahr-e-halala ne jigar ke tukday.<br />
Aur Qasim ke liye (x2).<br />
Kamsini mein hai ye gham sham-e-ghareebi jaisa.<br />
Wa Hasan-e-sabz qaba.</p>
<p>Bay gunah… (x2)</p>
<p>5. Ghar se ik baar janaza jo nikal jata hai.<br />
Phir wo kab aata hai (x2).<br />
Ye woh taboot hai phir laut ke jo ghar ayaa.<br />
Wa Hasan-e-sabaz qaba.</p>
<p>Bay gunah… (x2)</p>
<p>6. Sheh ne us waqt jo Ghazi ko na roka hota (x2).<br />
Hashr ho jata bapa (x2).<br />
Teer aatay na janazay pe tumharay Mola.<br />
Wa Hasan-e-sabz qaba.</p>
