<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">**Aao Chha Kayaa Baba**</span></p>
<p>Faryad karay thi baba khe.<br/>Hin sham-e-gareeba je ban mein.<br/>Jehe bhair ja arha bhayan hojun.<br/>Oha bhair akeli karbal mein.<br/>Faryad karay thi.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa baba majboor aa Zainab.<br/>Main kya karoon baba majboor hai Zainab (x2).<br/>Aao chha kayaa baba majboor aa Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x2)</p>
<p>1. Zainab aa akeli aain sham-e-gareeban (x2).<br/>Gurbat jo andhero aain Sani-e-Zehra (x2).<br/>Tatheer ji maalik (x2) aain be rida Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x2)</p>
<p>2. Ye sham-e-ghareeban hum be sar-o-saamaa (x2).<br/>Laashoan se bhara hai ye khoon bhara maidaa (x2).<br/>Ye ranj-o-masaayib (x2) dil par sahay Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x2)</p>
<p>3. Ik roz mein baba ujda hai mera ghar (x2).<br/>Atthara baradar koi nahi sar par (x2).<br/>Noha karay Zainab (x2) matam karay Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x2)</p>
<p>4. Baba mere bachhay ban mein gayay maaray (x2).<br/>Veeran meray saaray maqtal ko sidhaaray (x2).<br/>Pyaaro se bichhad kar (x2) kaisay rahay Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x2)</p>
<p>5. Behta raha darya bachhay rahay pyaasay (x2).<br/>Kehtay hain kahani tootay huay koozay (x2).<br/>Paani ki talab mein (x2) aansoo peeyay Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x2)</p>
<p>6. Gardan pe chhuri thi main dar pe khadi thi (x2).<br/>Khoo behta tha Sheh ka main dekh rahi thi (x2).<br/>Wo dard ka manzar (x2) kis se kahay Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x2)</p>
<p>7. Rehan ka noha aye Sani-e-Zehra (x2).<br/>Har bazm-e-aza main ho gham ka khulasa (x2).<br/>Girta hua aansoo (x2) kehta rahay Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x2)</p>
<p>Main kya karoon baba majboor hai Zainab (x2).<br/>Aao chha kaya baba majboor aa Zainab.</p>
<p>Aao chha kayaa… (x4)</p>
