<p style="font-size:18px">**Karbala Le Chal Mujhe**</p>



<p>Karbala Karbala Karbala aye karbala. (x2)</p>



<p>Jaanay waalay karbala ke karbala le chal mujhe (x4).<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala ke karbala le chal mujhay (x2).<br/>Roza-e-Shabbir par doonga dua le chal mujhay.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala ke karbala le chal mujhay.  (x2) </p>



<p>Hai jahaa’n ganj-e-shaheeda’n hai jahaa’n nehr-e-furat  (x2).<br/>Jis jaga sayyad mera pyaasa raha le chal mujhe.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala… (x2) </p>



<p>Jis jaga jaagi thi kismat Hurr ki wo dekhunga main  (x2).<br/>Aasiyo’n par kaisay hoti hai ataa le chal mujhe.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala… (x2)</p>



<p>Is kadar nadaar hoon zaad-e-safar kuch bhi nahi  (x2).<br/>Dosh par apni bitha kar aye hawa le chal mujhe.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala… (x2)</p>



<p>Jis jaga Abbas ke baazu kalam ho kar giray (x2).<br/>Us jaga main bhi karoo’n maatam zara le chal mujhe.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala… (x2)</p>



<p>Dil ulajh kar aa gaya barchi mein Akbar ka jaha’n x2.<br/>Dil waha’n main bhi karoo’n apna fida le chal mujhe.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala… x2</p>



<p>Jal gayay sham-e-gharibaa’n mein jaha’n khaimay tamam (x2).<br/>Jis jaga nannha sa ik jhoola jala le chal mujhe.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala…  (x2)</p>



<p>Haaye wo maqtal jahaa’n baali Sakina raat mein  (x2).<br/>Dhoondti phirti thi seena baap ka le chal mujhe.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala…  (x2)</p>



<p>Sarwar-o-Rehan har majlis mein kartay hain dua  (x2).<br/>Haq waheen nohoan ka ab hoga ada le chal mujhe.<br/>Jaanay waalay karbala…  (x2)</p>



<p>Jaanay waalay karbala ke karbala le chal mujhay  (x2).<br/>Roza-e-Shabbir par doonga dua le chal mujhe.</p>



<p>Jaanay waalay karbala ke, karbala le chal mujhe  (x2)</p>
