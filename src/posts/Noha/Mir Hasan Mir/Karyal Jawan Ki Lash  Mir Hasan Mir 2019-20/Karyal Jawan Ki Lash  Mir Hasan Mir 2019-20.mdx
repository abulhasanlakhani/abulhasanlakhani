<p>Karyal jawan ki lash.</p>



<p>Akbar. Haaye Akbar (x2). Akbar. Haaye Akbar.<br/><br/>Ghoray se gir ke jab Ali Akbar ne di sadaa.<br/>Apni kamar ko thaam ke Shabbir ne kaha.<br/>Karyal jawan ki lash uthanay chalay hain hum.<br/>Aray seedhi ho aye kamar tujhay Abbas ki kasam.</p>



<p>Kadhyal jawa’n ki laash uthanay chalay hain hum.<br/>Seedhi ho aye kamar tujhay Abbas ki kasam. </p>



<p>Ye imtiha’n zaeefi mein mujhko nibhaana hai.<br/>Akbar ko le ke khaima-e-Zainab mein jaana hai.<br/>Rastay mein ladhkhadhayain na Shabbir ke kadam.</p>
