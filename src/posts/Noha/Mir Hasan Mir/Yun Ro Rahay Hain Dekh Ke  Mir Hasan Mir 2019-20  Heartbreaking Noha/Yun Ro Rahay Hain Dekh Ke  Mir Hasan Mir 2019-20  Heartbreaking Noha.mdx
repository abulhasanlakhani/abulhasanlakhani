<p class="has-medium-font-size">**Yun Ro Rahay Hain Dekh Ke Zainab ko Murtaza.**</p>



<p>Haaye haaye haaye haaye.<br/>Haaye Zainab Haaye Ali.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay hain dekh ke Zainab ko Murtaza.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay… (x2)</p>



<p>Shaayad khayaal aaya hai baazar-e-shaam ka.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay… (x2)</p>



<p>Zainab ke thay ye bayn Madinay chalo Hussain (x2).<br/>Kufa hamaaray rehnay ke qaabil nahi raha.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay… (x2)</p>



<p>Sajjad bayn kartay hain daada hain khush naseeb (x2).<br/>Betoa’n ne mil ke baap ko gusl-o-kafan diya.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay… (x2)</p>



<p>Pehla ye imtihan hai Ghazi ke sabr ka (x2).<br/>Abbas chup hain saamnay qaatil hai baap ka.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay… (x2)</p>



<p>Zainab na ro abhi mujhay aa jaayega sukoon (x2).<br/>Rumaal laa ke baandh do sar par Batool ka.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay… (x2)</p>



<p>Ik bibi chal rahi hai janaazay ke saath saath (x2).<br/>Lagta hai qabr chodh ke aayi hain Fatima.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay… (x2)</p>



<p>Maula kyun khaak aap ne daali hai zakhm par (x2).<br/>Kya yaad aa gayi hai musallay pe karbala.</p>



<p>Yun ro rahay… (x2)</p>



<p>Darbaar-e-shaam jaaonga Zainab main teray saath (x2).<br/>Akbar ye keh ke ho gayay khaamosh Murtaza.</p>
