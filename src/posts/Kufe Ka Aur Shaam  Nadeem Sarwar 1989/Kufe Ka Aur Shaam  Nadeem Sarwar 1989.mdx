<p>Kufe ka aur shaam ka manzar (x2).<br />
Yaad jab aa jata hai baba.<br />
Dil mera phat jaata hai baba.<br />
Kufe ka aur shaam ka manzar.</p>
<p>1. Kabr pe Shabbir ki kartay thay Abid buka (x2).<br />
Ho gayi kya kya jafa sun ye shah-e-karbala (x2).<br />
Sar pe kisi ke na thi chaadar.<br />
Yaad jab aa jata hai baba dil mera phat jaata hai baba.<br />
kufe ka aur shaam…</p>
<p>2. Aik rasand mein bandhay chotay badoan ke galay (x2).<br />
Barsar-e-darbar sab is tarhan laaye gaye (x2).<br />
Kothon se barsatay thay pathar.<br />
Yaad jab aa jata hai baba dil mera phat jaata hai baba.<br />
Kufe ka aur shaam…</p>
<p>3. Kufe ke baazaar mein majma-e kuffar mein (x2).<br />
Aik tamasha sa tha aal-e-sitamgaar mein (x2).<br />
Aisay mein maa behnain khulay sar.<br />
Yaad jab aa jata hai baba dil mera phat jaata hai baba<br />
Kufe ka aur…</p>
<p>4. Tauq se hargiz na tha baba mera sar jhuka (x2).<br />
Hathkadi aur bedi ka koi mujhe gham na tha (x2).<br />
Arey dekha nahi jaata tha manzar.<br />
Yaad jab aa jata hai baba dil mera phat jaata hai baba.<br />
Kufe ka aur…</p>
<p>5. Arey baali Sakina ka gham dil ye nahi bhoolta (x2).<br />
Sab ke the shaanay bandhay us ka bandha tha gala (x2).<br />
Arey dard se wo roti thi muztar.<br />
Yaad jab aa jata hai baba dil mera phat jaata hai baba.<br />
Kufe ka aur…</p>
<p>6. Meri phuphi jaan thi balway mein jo sar khulay (x2).<br />
Naizay se ammu mere farsh pe gir padtay thay (x2).<br />

Yaad jab aa jata hai baba dil mera phat jaata hai baba.<br />
Kufe ka aur…</p>
<p>7. Le ke gayay jab hamain bazm-e-sitamgaar mein (x2).<br />
Saat-sau kursi nasheen baithay thay darbaar mein (x2).<br />

Kufe ka aur…</p>
<p>8. Jab kiya Sajjad se aa ke kisi ne sawaal (x2).<br />
Guzra kahan par Imam aap ko zyaada malaal (x2).<br />
Arey kehtay thay ash-shaam muqarrar.<br />
Yaad jab aa jata hai baba dil mera phat jaata hai baba.<br />
Kufe ka aur…</p>
