<p class="has-normal-font-size">Midhat-E-Haider mere lab par zara aanay to do. (x2)<br/>Daad mujhko phir tumhara dil agar maanay to do.<br/>Midhat-E-Haider mere lab par zara aanay to do.</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Hosh main aajayengi fauran sipah-e-bad sifaat. (x2)<br/>Tum mohibbaan-e-Ali ko josh mein aanay to do.<br/>Midhat-E Haider (A.s.)…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Khud ba khud phirtay nazar aayengay tufaanoan ke rukh. (x2)<br/>Tum fizaa main parcham-e-Abbas lehraanay to do.<br/>Midhat-E-Haider (A.s.)…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kaun roya kaun soya sab pata chal Jaayega. (x2)<br/>Tum shab-e-hijrat ka kissa hum ko dohranay to do.<br/>Midhat-E-Haider (A.s.)…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Dekhna kaisa tawaana hoga ye laagar mareez. (x2)<br/>Majlis-e-Sheh ka tabarruk tum isay khaanay to do.<br/>Midhat-E-Haider (A.S.)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Saaf aayega nazar maidaan taa hadd-e-nazar. (x2)<br/>Hazrat-E-Abbas ko maidaan main aanay to do.<br/>Midhat-E-Haider (A.s.)…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Daad mujhko phir tumhara dil agar maanay to do.<br/>Midhat-E-Haider mere lab par zara aanay to do.</p>
