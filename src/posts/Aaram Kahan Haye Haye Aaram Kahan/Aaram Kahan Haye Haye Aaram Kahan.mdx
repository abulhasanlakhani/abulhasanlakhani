<p class="has-normal-font-size">Aaram kahaan Haye Haye Aaram Kahan.<br/>Kisht-e-aalam mein Zainab ko bhala. <br/>Aaram kahan haye haye…</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Qatl Shabbir bhi Abbas bhi Akbar bhi huay.<br/>Kat gayay Aon-o-Muhammad ke bhi maqtal main galay.<br/>Lasha-e-Ibn-e-Hasan ho gaya pamaal-e-jafa.<br/>Aaram kahan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kisht-e-aalam mein Zainab ko bhala. <br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)  </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Teer jab Asghar-e-maasoom ki gardan mein laga.<br/>Deti thi khaimay ke dar par khari Baano ye sada.<br/>Bin teray chayn milay kaisay meray maah laqa.<br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Chid gayi mashk-e-Sakina lab-e-darya afsos.<br/>Be kafan khaak pe hai sher ka laasha afsos.<br/>Pyaasay bachon ka koi chahnay waala na raha.<br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kisht-e-aalam mein Zainab ko bhala. <br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Chin gayi sar se rida jal gaya asbaab tamaam.<br/>Baad-e-Shabbir namudaar hui kaisi ye sham.<br/>Khoon kanoa’n se baha bachi ka daaman bhi jala.<br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kisht-e-aalam mein Zainab ko bhala. <br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Sar shaheedon ke sar-e-nok-e-sina qaid haram.<br/>Aik do ka nahi seenay mein bahattar ka hai gham.<br/>Bediyaan pehnay huay laaghar-o-beemar chala.<br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kisht-e-aalam mein Zainab ko bhala. <br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Koi maara gaya talwaar se naizay se koi.<br/>Laash maqtal mein kisi dulha ki pamaal hui.<br/>Ho gayay bazoo-e-Abbas-e-Alamdar juda.<br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kisht-e-aalam mein Zainab ko bhala. <br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Ro ro kehti hai Sakina meray ammo aao.<br/>Mujh ko maray na laeen aa ke inhain samjhaao.<br/>Kyun nahi suntay ho deti hoon tumhain kab se sada.<br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kisht-e-aalam mein Zainab ko bhala. <br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Bachay jo khaak pe girtay hain kuchal jaatay hain.<br/>Gham se dil maoon ke seenay se nikal jaatay hain.<br/>Koi sunta nahi bewaaon ki faryad-o-buka.<br/>Aaram kahaan… (x3)</p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Kisht-e-aalam mein Zainab ko bhala. <br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3) </p>



<p class="has-normal-font-size">Ankh roti hay kalam likhta hai noha Rehan.<br/>Hai karam mujh pe shah-e-Karbobala ka Rehan.<br/>Noha jab tak na likhoon roz shah-e-dee’n ka naya.<br/>Aaraam kahan… (x3)</p>
