<p style="font-size:18px">Mere wird-e-lab hai Nabi Nabi.<br/>Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi. <br/>Mera dil makaan-e-Habeeb hai.<br/><br/>Main mareez-e-ishq-e-Rasool hoon.<br/>Main mareez-e-ishq-e-Rasool hoon. <br/>Wo Habeeb mera tabeeb hai.<br/><br/>Mere wird…  </p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-background has-very-light-gray-background-color">1. Mera us galee se hai raabta.<br/>Jahaan sar jhukatay hain ambiyaa.<br/><br/>Mera us galee se hai raabta.<br/>Jahaan sar jhukatay hain ambiyaa. <br/><br/>Jahaan rehmaton ka nuzool hai.<br/>Jahaan rehmaton ka nuzool hai. <br/>Wo jo arsh-e-haq ke kareeb hai.</p>



<p style="font-size:18px">Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi.<br/>Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi. <br/>Mera dil makaan-e-Habeeb hai. <br/>Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi. </p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-background has-very-light-gray-background-color">2. Main bada ameer-o-kabeee hoon.<br/>Shah-e-do sara ka aseer hoon.<br/><br/>Main bada ameer-o-kabeee hoon.<br/>Main bada ameer-o-kabeee hoon. <br/>Shah-e-do sara ka aseer hoon. <br/><br/>Dar-e-Mustafa ka fakeer hoon.<br/>Dar-e-Mustafa ka fakeer hoon. <br/>Mera rif’aton main naseeb hai.</p>



<p style="font-size:18px">Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi.<br/>Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi. <br/>Mera dil makaan-e-Habeeb hai. <br/>Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi. </p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-background has-very-light-gray-background-color">3. Main gham-o-alam mein hoon mubtila<br/>Koi kya karega meri dawa.<br/><br/>Main gham-o-alam mein hoon mubtila<br/>Koi kya karega meri dawa. <br/><br/>Mera do jahaan mein tere siwaa.<br/>Mera do jahaan mein tere siwaa. <br/>Shah aur kaun tabeeb hai.</p>



<p style="font-size:18px">Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi.<br/>Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi. <br/>Mera dil makaan-e-Habeeb hai. <br/>Mere wird e lab hai Nabi Nabi. </p>



<p style="font-size:18px" class="has-background has-very-light-gray-background-color">4. Wo bhi khoob din thay malaaika.<br/>Mere saamnay thay jo sar nigoon.<br/><br/>Wo bhi khoob din thay malaaika.<br/>Mere saamnay thay jo sar nigoon. <br/><br/>Wo mera urooj-e-kamaal tha.<br/>Wo mera urooj-e-kamaal tha. <br/>Ye zawaal kitna ajeeb hai.</p>
