<p>Ye chaukhat choom kar ki hai dua har baar mashhad mein. (x2)</p>
<p>Ilahi theher jaaye waqt ki raftaar mashhad mein.<br />
Ye chaukhat choom kar ki hai dua har baar mashad mein.</p>
<p>1. Janazay to bohot aatay huay dekhe magar maine. (x2)<br />
Nahi dekhay kahin bhi maut ke aasaar mashad mein.</p>
<p>2. Suna hai khud shifa denay ko aatay hain wahan maula.<br />
So ab khud jaa ke main ho jaaunga bemaar mashad mein.</p>
<p>3. Raza ke husn par Yusuf ko biktay dekhti dunya. (x2)<br />
Agar hota kahin jo misr ka bazaar mashad mein</p>
<p>4. Suna kar manqabat Maula ki main un se dua lunga.<br />
Milengay jab kabhi bhi Meesam-e-Tammar mashad mein.</p>
<p>5. Wo chahay ghar ki surat ho ya chahay qabr ki surat. (x2)<br />
Khuda wanda bana de mir ka darbaar mashad mein.</p>
<p>Ye chaukhat choom kar ki hai dua har baar mashhad mein.</p>
