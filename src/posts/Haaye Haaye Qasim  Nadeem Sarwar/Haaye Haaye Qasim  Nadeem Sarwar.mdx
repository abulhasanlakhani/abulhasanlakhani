<p>Haaye Haaye Qasim haaye Qasim (x2).<br />
Haaye Haaye Qasim haaye Qasim (x2).</p>
<p>Qaidi ban kar paaband-e-rasand laashon se jo guzray ahl-e-haram.<br />
Har bibi apnay pyaaron se hoti thi vida karkay maatam.<br />
Ek bibi Fatima bint-e-Hasan pamaal badan Qasim ki behan.<br />
Kisi ek jaan se rukhsat na hui (x2).<br />
Tukdon mein jo tha bhai ka badan.</p>
<p>Kabhi is tukday par haaye Qasim kabhi us tukday par haaye Qasim.<br />
Teri bhain gharib noo nayi bhulna (x2).<br />
Tere qatal da manzar haaye Qasim.<br />
Haaye haaye… (x2)</p>
<p>1) Kabhi sehra dekh key rooti thi kabhi ghash khake girjati thi (x2).<br />
Paamaal hua tha bhai jahaan (x2). Us khoon pe girkar haaye Qasim.</p>
<p>Haaye haaye… (x2)</p>
<p>2) Wich rassiyan de paaband hui nayi bhain garib da vas koi (x2).<br />
Hunday hath aazad te marjaandi (x2). Tera matam kar kar haaye Qasim.</p>
<p>Haaye haaye… (x2)</p>
<p>3) Kis shaan se tum dulha thay banay kis shaan se thi baraat chali (x2).<br />
Bethi rahi dulhan hujray mein (x2). Aaye na palat kar haaye qasim.</p>
<p>Haaye haaye… (x2)</p>
<p>4) Farwa ne kaha beta Qasim keh dena daadi Zehra se (x2).<br />
Ghurbat ki kamayi tukdon mein (x2) Dedi hai saja kar haaye Qasim.</p>
<p>Haaye haaye Qasim… (x2)</p>
<p>5) Sanu raas na aayi karbobala Tu baanj kafan main baanj rida (x2).<br />
Tere qatal de baad layeena ne (x2) Meri lut layi chaadar haaye Qasim.</p>
<p>Haaye haaye Qasim… (x2)</p>
<p>6) Har saath muharrum ko mehndi teri yaad me Qasim uthti hai (x2).<br />
Dulha dulha Qasim dulha (x2) Hota hai labon par haaye Qasim.</p>
<p>Haaye haaye Qasim… (x2)</p>
