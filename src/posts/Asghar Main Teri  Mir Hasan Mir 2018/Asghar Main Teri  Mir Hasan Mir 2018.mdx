<p>**Asghar Main Teri Maa Hun**</p>
<p>Aaja Asghar Aaja Asghar (x2).<br />
Aaja Asghar Aaja Asghar (x2).</p>
<p>Asghar main teri maa hun (x2).<br />
Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main teri maa hun.</p>
<p>Meray laadlay kahan ho (x2).<br />
Seenay tumhain lagaaon.<br />
Asghar main…</p>
<p>Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main…</p>
<p>1. Beta tumhara qaatil mere saamnay khada hai.<br />
Janay kyun hans raha hai. Chaadar bhi lut chuki hai.<br />
Muu kis tarhan chupaaon. Asghar main…</p>
<p>Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main…</p>
<p>2. Tere jhoolay ko Sakina ro ro ke choomti hai.<br />
Tujhe ghar mein dhoondti hai. Teri maut ki khabar main.<br />
Kaisay usay sunaaon. Asghar main…</p>
<p>Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main…</p>
<p>3. Meri jaan ja rahi hai ye soch soch Asghar.<br />
Is phool se galay par ye teer kaisay jhela.<br />
Behnoan ko kya bataaon. Asghar main…</p>
<p>Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main…</p>
<p>4. Naizay se gir raha hai nanna sa sar tumhara.<br />
Dil rota hai hamara. Mere haath to bandhay hain.<br />
Sar kis tarhan uthaaon. Asghar main…</p>
<p>Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main…</p>
<p>5. Jhoolay ke saath beta mere haath jal gaye hain.<br />
Zaalim bhi hans rahay hain. Teri aakhri nishaani.<br />
Kis tarhan se bachaaon. Asghar main…</p>
<p>Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main…</p>
<p>6. Jab raat hogi ran mein ho jaayega andhera.<br />
Darna nahi aye beta. Daadi kahaingi tum se.<br />
Aa main tujhe sulaaon. Asghar main…</p>
<p>Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main…</p>
<p>7. Tum raat ko hamesha suntay thay maa se lori.<br />
Ab neend aati hogi. Phir godh mein lita kar.<br />
Naad-e-Ali sunaaon. Asghar main…</p>
<p>Aa jao chaand meray teri pyaas main bujhaaon.<br />
Asghar main teri maa hoon (x3)</p>
