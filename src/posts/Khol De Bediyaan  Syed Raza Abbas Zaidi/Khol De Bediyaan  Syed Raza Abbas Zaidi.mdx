<p>Jo qaid ho ke chala kaarwan-e-Aal-e-Nabi.<br />
Nazar Sakina ki Sajjad-e-na tawaan pe padi.<br />
Ke taaziyanay bhi jis ko laga rahay thay shaki.<br />
Tadap ke is tarhan shimr-e-layeen se kehnay lagi.</p>
<p>Khol de bediyaan bhai beemar hai (x2).</p>
<p>1. Soch behr-e-Khuda ye hai Aal-e-aba (x2).<br />
Yuun na durray laga bhai beemar hai.<br />
Khol de bediyaan bhai beemar hai.</p>
<p>2. Pehlay hi is ko gham kya diye tu ne kam (x2).<br />
Ab na kar ye sitam bhai beemar hai.<br />
Khol de…</p>
<p>3. Kya milay ga tujhay is ko yuun maar ke (x2).<br />
Ye aziyyat na de bhai beemar hai.<br />
Khol de…</p>
<p>4. Dekh aye badgumaan kar na ye sakhtiyaan (x2).<br />
De de is ko amaa bhai beemar hai.<br />
Khol de…</p>
<p>5. Jism hai khoo se tar ab to kuch rehm kar (x2).<br />
Ye hai raah-e-safar bhai beemar hai.<br />
Khol de…</p>
<p>6. Khoon rota raha Shabbar-e-be nawa (x2).<br />
Sun ke us ki sada bhai beemar hai.<br />
Khol de bediyaan bhai beemar hai.</p>
