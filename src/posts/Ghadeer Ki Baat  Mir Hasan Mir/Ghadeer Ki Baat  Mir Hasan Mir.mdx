<p>**Ghadeer Ki Baat**</p>
<p>Haan mere dostoan (x2).</p>
<p>Haan mere dostoan ghadeer ki baat (x2).</p>
<p>Haan mere dostoan ghadeer kii baat.<br />
Hai munaafiq ke dil mein teer ki baat.</p>
<p>1. Ye to zikr-e-Ameer hai warna (x2).<br />
Kaun sunta tha is faqeer ki baat (x2).</p>
<p>2. Ishq badhta gaya faqeeroan ka.<br />
Jab bhi ki Hazrat-e-Ameer ki baat (x2).</p>
<p>3. Pooch mat mujhse wajh-e-bugz-e-Ali (x2).<br />
Is mein aayegi maa ke sheer ki baat (x2).</p>
<p>4. Aik parda nasheen tak aayegi (x2).<br />
Jab chalay gi kisi shareer ki baat (x2).</p>
<p>5. Aap ke aashiqoan ko khalti hai.<br />
Ya Nabi Aap ke wazir ki baat (x2).</p>
<p>Haan mere dostoan ghadeer kii baat.</p>
