<p>Aik najaf mein mera Ali hai</p>
<p><div class="one_half"></p>
<p>Aik najaf mein mera Ali hai.<br />
Aik Ali Iran mein hai.<br />
Hum palla donoan ki sakhaawat.<br />
Kudrat ke meezan mein hai.</p>
<p>1. Mola Raza aur Mehdi-e-Deen mein.<br />
Farq yehi mamooli hai.<br />
Ik Quran khula rakha hai.<br />
Aik abhi juzdaan mein hai.</p>
<p>2. Mashhad un ke dum se muqaddas.<br />
Shehar hua hai sheharoan mein.<br />
Jaisay ke Rahman ka sura.<br />
Dulhan sa Quran mein hai.</p>
<p>Aik najaf mein mera Ali hai.</p>
